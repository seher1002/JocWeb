Popular Card Games

War

A simple and fun adaptation of the card game for two players. Each player draws a card, and the player with the highest card wins the round. The goal is to win all the cards.

Sort Cards

An interactive web application that allows users to draw cards from a virtual deck and sort them in ascending order.
